Mission Statement

Ontario Physical Therapy and Wellness will be the provider of choice for physical therapy and wellness services in Malheur County and the surrounding area by using clinically proven interventions, to achieve the highest functional outcomes, in the most cost efficient manner.

Core Values

Vision

We understand that the healthcare field is a constantly evolving marketplace. We will have the vision to guide this evolution, adapt to change, and overcome challenges proactively.

Integrity

We commit ourselves to the highest moral and ethical standards within our organization and with those whom we do business with.

Talent

A talented staff is the engine that drives our organization. We strive to create an environment that attracts gifted personnel, that rewards progressive action, and that encourages continual acquisition of skills and education.

Leadership

We shape our community and our profession by continuous innovation, community involvement, and by implementation of our core values and vision.

Vision

Our goal is improving the quality of life of our customers. Never content, our pursuit of this goal will propel our influence from locally to regionally and beyond.
